Privacy Policy for Kana Quick

Last updated: November 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kana Quick. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in our Japanese language learning application.

2. Information We Collect

Kana Quick is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device using AsyncStorage:

  • Character practice statistics (accuracy rates, practice counts)
  • Your progress and performance data
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

3. Advertising

Kana Quick displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain information for advertising purposes.

Google AdMob

AdMob may collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Ad interaction data
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)

We have configured our app to request non-personalized ads only. For more information about how Google uses data, please visit:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Privacy Information

4. Permissions

Kana Quick requests minimal permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to display advertisements
  • Storage: Used to save your progress locally on your device

5. Data Security

All your learning progress and statistics are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. If you uninstall the app, all local data will be removed from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

Kana Quick is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app only stores anonymous practice statistics locally on the device.

7. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into Kana Quick is Google AdMob for displaying advertisements. We do not share any data with third parties beyond what AdMob collects automatically (as described in Section 3).

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal information on our servers:

  • You can delete all your local data by uninstalling the app
  • You can clear your progress data by clearing the app's storage in your device settings
  • No data is transmitted to us, so there is nothing for us to delete or modify
